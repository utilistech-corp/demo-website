Who We Are

About Emberline

A women-founded consultancy led by senior strategists and creatives in London and New York.

Our Story

Built for brands that
refuse to blend in

Emberline was founded by two creative directors who spent fifteen years at top-tier agencies and kept seeing the same pattern: brilliant briefs diluted by layers, handoffs, and misaligned incentives.

We built Emberline to be the opposite. A consultancy where the people in the room are the people doing the work. Where senior experience isn't a line on a pitch deck — it's every conversation, every concept, every pixel.

Our clients are founders, CEOs, and brand leaders who want craft without the agency tax. People who value partnership over production lines.
