How We Work

WHAT WORKING WITH US ACTUALLY LOOKS LIKE.

Every engagement follows the same principles: understand first, build second, measure everything. Here's our process and three examples of the kind of work we do.

Our Process

FOUR STEPS. NO SURPRISES.

01

Discovery

We start with your business, not the technology. A focused session to understand your workflows, pain points, data landscape, and what success actually looks like for you.

02

Assessment

We map what's viable, what's valuable, and what needs sorting first. You get an honest view of AI readiness, risks, and a prioritised list of opportunities.

03

Build & Deploy

We build the solution — whether that's a custom AI tool, a workflow automation, or a governed Copilot rollout — integrated into your existing environment.

04

Measure & Support

We track the outcomes we agreed upfront. Monthly support is available to optimise, troubleshoot, and identify new opportunities as your confidence grows.

Example Engagements

THE KIND OF WORK WE DO.

These are representative examples of typical engagements. Every project is different, but this gives you a sense of our approach and what you can expect.

💼

AI Readiness Assessment

Financial services firm, ~50 employees

The Scenario

A mid-size advisory firm knows AI matters but has no clear roadmap. They're worried about compliance, unsure which processes are suitable, and don't want to waste budget on the wrong tools.

Our Approach

Half-day discovery workshop, process mapping across core workflows, data readiness review, and a prioritised roadmap. Governance requirements and compliance considerations addressed upfront — before any tools are selected.

Typical Outcomes

  • • Clear 6-month AI adoption plan with priorities
  • • 3–5 high-impact processes identified for automation
  • • Governance framework in place before any AI tools deployed
  • • Team aligned on where AI fits — and where it doesn't
schedule Typical timeline: 1–2 weeks
⚖️

Custom Document Intelligence

Professional services firm, ~120 employees

The Scenario

Staff spending 15+ hours a week manually reviewing and summarising client documents. Inconsistent output, human error, and a bottleneck that affects fee earner utilisation and client turnaround.

Our Approach

Build a custom AI tool that reads incoming documents, categorises them by type, extracts key facts, and produces summaries — with human-in-the-loop review built in. Full audit trail for compliance and client confidence.

Typical Outcomes

  • • 60%+ reduction in document processing time
  • • Consistent output quality — no variation between staff
  • • Full audit trail for compliance and client review
  • • Staff freed up for higher-value advisory work
schedule Typical timeline: 4–6 weeks
🛍️

AI Training & Adoption

Retail or e-commerce business, ~30 employees

The Scenario

The team has heard about AI but has no idea how to use it safely or effectively. No policies in place, worried about data risks, and uncertain which tools are appropriate for their work.

Our Approach

Full-day training workshop with hands-on exercises using real workflows. We create an approved tools list and AI usage policy aligned with data safety requirements. Follow-up sessions measure adoption and identify new opportunities.

Typical Outcomes

  • • 80%+ of team actively using AI tools within 4 weeks
  • • Clear data safety guidelines embedded in everyday practice
  • • New process improvements identified by staff themselves
  • • Confidence and momentum to continue improving
schedule Typical timeline: 1 day workshop + 2 weeks follow-up
