Our Work

A curated selection of brands we've had the privilege of shaping — from strategy through to execution.

Brand Strategy & Identity

Northvane Capital

Positioning Visual Identity Website Collateral

Northvane needed to attract a younger generation of high-net-worth clients without alienating their established base. We repositioned the brand around the idea of "quiet confidence" — a visual and verbal identity that signals substance, not flash.

The result: a 40% increase in inbound enquiries from under-40 clients within six months of launch.

Verbal & Visual Identity

Aura Wellness

Brand World Naming Tone of Voice Digital

Aura came to us pre-launch with a vision and a prototype — but no brand. We built everything from the name up: a premium wellness identity that feels warm without being soft, and scientific without being clinical.

Aura launched in three European markets simultaneously, with press coverage in Vogue, Wallpaper*, and Monocle within the first quarter.

Digital & Campaign

Helios Engineering

Creative Platform Website Campaign Motion

Helios had the technical expertise but their brand didn't reflect it. We developed a creative platform — "Engineered to Inspire" — that repositioned them as a design-led engineering company, not just another consultancy.

The rebrand supported a successful Series B raise and helped Helios win three enterprise contracts within the first year.

Brand Strategy

Verso Publishing

Positioning Narrative Identity System

Verso is an independent publisher with serious literary ambitions. They needed a brand identity that could hold its own alongside the majors while staying true to their indie roots and editorial voice.

We created a flexible identity system anchored in bold typography and a restrained colour palette that lets the writing speak first.

Full Rebrand

Stratum Architecture

Strategy Identity Website Launch

Stratum's brand had been untouched for fifteen years. We led a complete rebrand — from strategic positioning through to a new website, environmental graphics, and a launch campaign that reintroduced them to the market.

The firm won two international design awards within months of the rebrand going live.
